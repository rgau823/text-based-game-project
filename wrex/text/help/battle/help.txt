=================== Battle Help ===================
This is the combat menu when you engage in battle
with enemies.

The Commands:
(A)ttack    - Attacks the enemy/boss on that level
(G)ift      - Gift the enemy a gift
(F)lee      - Flees the fight. It's a random chance.
(B)ackpack  - Check what's in your backpack
(I)nfo      - Gives info on the enemy/boss on that level
(H)elp      - Brings the help menu
